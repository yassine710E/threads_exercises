Objective
Practice serialized logging: each log line must be atomic — no interleaved output from concurrent threads.

What to implement
Each thread logs [timestamp_ms] coder N started and [timestamp_ms] coder N finished. Use a dedicated log mutex. Timestamps come from gettimeofday.

Validation
No two log lines are interleaved. Timestamps are monotonically non-decreasing within each thread.
